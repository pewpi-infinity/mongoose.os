# Gallium-Based Liquid Metals (GLMs) — Biomedical Pulse Systems

## 1. Gallium as a Pulsing Micro-Variant

### Thermal/Electrical Pumping
- High thermal conductivity — can be pumped via electrical and magnetic fields
- Used to create **electronic blood vessels** with excellent patency in animal models

### Heat Regulation
- Phase change (melting/solidifying) near room temperature
- Functions as responsive "soft heater" inside the body — absorbs/releases heat

### Sensing and Feedback
- Integrated with vascular stents as **bioelectrodes**
- Monitors **blood pressure** and **intimal hyperplasia** in real-time
- Sends signals back to external monitoring systems

Sources: OAE Publishing, NIH

## 2. Antiviral & Medical Applications

### COVID-19 / SARS-CoV-2
- **Gallium maltolate** disrupts replication of SARS-CoV-2
- Source: NIH — "Gallium Components-Based Drug Delivery: A Potential Treatment for COVID-19"

### Nuclear Medicine — Gallium Scans
- Radioactive gallium injected into blood
- Attaches to proteins, collects where cells rapidly divide
- Locates inflammation and cancer

### Blood Compatibility
- High biocompatibility with human blood
- Does not damage red blood cells
- Does not significantly alter blood components

Source: Johns Hopkins Medicine

## 3. Cryobiology — "Impossible to Shut Off"

Liquid metals explored for **eternal preservation** of biological resources:
- Enhance heat transfer during preservation
- Regulate ice crystal formation
